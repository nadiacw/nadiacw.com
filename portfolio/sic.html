SÓNAR INNOVATION CHALLENGE

A week long hackathon with Inflight VR at Sónar music festival

Keywords: hackathon, virtual reality, inflight entertainment

As part of Sónar+D 2017 (Barcelona), I participated in the Sónar Innovation Challenge. It challenged multidisciplinary teams to create an application or experience during the Sónar festival.

Our team was sponsored by Inflight VR and challenged us to create an VR in-flight specific experience. We chose to prototype a musical VR experience using the Leap Motion gesture controller, where users could grab predefined sounds and move them and modify them in VR, thus creating a fun and engaging interactive experience.
