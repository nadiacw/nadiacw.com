VIRTUAL, AUGMENTED AND MIXED REALITY

VR development at Visyon

Keywords: virtual reality, social issues awareness, games, advertisement

A few projects I was part of at Visyon. Most projects have been developed using Unity3D. The platforms used are Microsoft Hololens, HTC Vive, Google Daydream and Cardboard, Samsung Gear, Oculus Rift and Android and iOS mobile phones.

    A Walk Through Dementia - Android app development using Unity3D for Google Cardboard.
    Dignitas - Last Moments - Android app development using Unity3D for GearVR.
    SpecSavers Referee Challenge - Android app development using Unity3D for GearVR.
    La Vaca Que Juega - La Vaca Que Ríe - Augmented reality - Android and iOS development using Unity3D and HTML5/CSS. Development of several mini-games and general app functions. Augmented reality using Vuforia.
    Busabout 360º - Busabout - Android and iOS app development using Unity3D. App for Google Cardboard using Google VR SDK.
    + Other demos and prototypes for HTC Vive, Microsoft Hololens, etc.
